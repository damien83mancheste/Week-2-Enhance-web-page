The Rarest Mineral: Painite

Introduction to Painite

Painite is one of the rarest minerals on Earth. Discovered in Myanmar in the 1950s, it was once considered the world's rarest gemstone.

Composition of Painite

Painite is a borate mineral that contains aluminum, calcium, zirconium, and traces of vanadium and chromium, which give it a reddish-brown color.
